About Us

Learn More About Us

Ballard Mourning Assistance is dedicated to providing support and resources for individuals dealing with grief and loss. Our mission is to offer a compassionate space where people can find the help they need to navigate their emotional journey.

  • Evidence-based grief counseling programs
  • Online forums for peer support
  • Access to professional counselors via phone, chat, and email
  • A comprehensive library of resources on grief and mourning

Our services are designed to be accessible, convenient, and private, allowing you to seek help in a way that fits your needs. Whether you are dealing with the loss of a loved one, a significant life change, or supporting someone who is grieving, we are here to help.

Our Story

How We Started

Ballard Mourning Assistance was founded by a group of individuals who personally experienced the profound effects of loss and wanted to create a space for others to find solace and support. Our team is made up of professionals and volunteers who are passionate about helping people through their grief journey.

  • Founded in response to a lack of accessible grief support services
  • Grew from a small community initiative to a comprehensive support organization
  • Continues to expand services to meet the needs of a diverse population

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide a supportive environment for those dealing with loss. Over the years, we have developed a range of services and resources to help people at different stages of their grief journey, from immediate support following a loss to long-term counseling and community engagement.

Mission & Vision

Our Commitment

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide compassionate and accessible support for those experiencing grief and loss. We strive to empower individuals by offering a range of resources and services that cater to their emotional, mental, and physical well-being during their journey through grief.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where everyone has access to the support they need during times of grief. We aim to foster a community where individuals can share their experiences, find understanding, and receive the help they need to heal and move forward.
